# The Awakening - Questline Design

## Overview

A 13-quest storyline spanning two zones: **New Aeven** (major city hub) and the **Desert** (ancient buried civilization). Players uncover a growing magical threat and ultimately face **The Hollow King** - an ancient entity that consumes magic, sealed away centuries ago by the lost Aetheri civilization.

**Level range:** 20-40 (mid-game)
**Zones:** New Aeven (city), Desert (ruins/temples)
**Link between zones:** Loosely connected - shared Aetheri lore, but each zone's quests are playable independently

## Lore Foundation

### The Aetheri
A civilization that once flourished in the desert. Masters of magic, they built grand temples and cities. Their downfall came when they accidentally awakened (or created) **The Hollow King** - a being that feeds on magical energy, growing stronger the more power is used against it. The Aetheri sacrificed their civilization's magic to forge three **Binding Seals** to imprison the Hollow King underground. The effort drained them completely, and their civilization crumbled to dust and sand.

### The Hollow King
An entity that consumes magic. Cannot be killed by spells alone - magical attacks heal or empower it. It was sealed beneath the desert by three Binding Seals. Now, centuries later, the seals are weakening. Its influence causes magical disturbances that radiate outward along underground ley lines - reaching as far as New Aeven.

### The Seals
- **First Seal** - Beneath New Aeven (the city was unknowingly built on top of it)
- **Second Seal** - A desert temple, partially exposed
- **Third Seal** - The Aetheri Sanctum, deep underground in the desert

## Part 1: New Aeven Questline (6 Quests)

### Key NPCs

- **Archmage Yenara** - Head of New Aeven's mage college. Scholarly, determined, and increasingly alarmed. The main quest giver for the city chain.
- **City Guard Captain** - First point of contact. Practical, overwhelmed by the chaos.

### Quest 1: "City of Sparks"

**Given by:** City Guard Captain
**Level:** 20
**Summary:** Arrive in New Aeven to find magical chaos - enchanted lanterns exploding, animated objects attacking market-goers. The guard captain asks you to investigate three disturbance sites around the city.

**Objectives:**
- ReachLocation: Investigate the market district disturbance
- ReachLocation: Investigate the mage college courtyard disturbance
- ReachLocation: Investigate the city gate disturbance
- TalkTo: Report findings to Archmage Yenara

**Details:** At each site, the player finds a strange resonance - a humming emanating from the ground beneath the cobblestones. The disturbances grow stronger the deeper underground they originate.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP

---

### Quest 2: "Containment Protocol"

**Given by:** Archmage Yenara
**Level:** 22
**Summary:** The magical disturbances have animated objects throughout the market district. Yenara needs you to destroy them and collect the dampening crystals they drop so she can stabilize the area.

**Objectives:**
- KillMonster: Kill 12 Animated Constructs
- CollectItem: Collect 8 Dampening Crystals

**New Monsters:**
- **Animated Construct** (level 22-25) - Enchanted suits of armor, brooms, and crates brought to life by wild magic. Drop Dampening Crystals.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP, Magic XP

---

### Quest 3: "The Old Foundation"

**Given by:** Archmage Yenara
**Level:** 24
**Summary:** Yenara's research reveals New Aeven was built on top of something ancient. She sends you into the city's underground cisterns to investigate.

**Objectives:**
- ReachLocation: Enter the city cisterns (instance)
- KillMonster: Kill 8 Seal Wraiths
- ReachLocation: Find the source of the disturbance
- TalkTo: Report to Yenara

**Instance: New Aeven Cisterns**
- Underground water channels beneath the city
- Carved walls with unknown symbols become visible as you go deeper
- At the deepest point: a massive cracked seal radiating magical energy

**New Monsters:**
- **Seal Wraith** (level 24-26) - Spectral entities drawn to the broken magic seeping from the seal. Ghostly, translucent appearance.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP, Prayer XP

---

### Quest 4: "Words of the Sealed"

**Given by:** Archmage Yenara
**Level:** 25
**Summary:** Yenara partially translates the cistern inscriptions. They're warnings from the Aetheri: *"What sleeps beneath the sand must never wake. The seals hold. The seals must hold."* She needs a Resonance Lens to read more, requiring specific materials.

**Objectives:**
- CollectItem: Collect 5 Refined Quartz (from mining)
- CollectItem: Collect 3 Construct Cores (rare drop from Animated Constructs)
- TalkTo: Bring materials to the city enchanting station NPC
- TalkTo: Return the finished Resonance Lens to Yenara

**Details:** The Resonance Lens is a key quest item that carries through to the desert chain. It allows the reader to see Aetheri inscriptions that are invisible to the naked eye.

**Rewards:** Gold, Mining XP, Magic XP, Resonance Lens (quest item)

---

### Quest 5: "Tremors"

**Given by:** Archmage Yenara (triggered automatically after Quest 4)
**Level:** 27
**Summary:** An earthquake strikes New Aeven. A section of the city wall collapses and Sand Wraiths - creatures from the desert - pour through the breach. You must defend the city.

**Objectives:**
- KillMonster: Kill 15 Sand Wraiths
- ReachLocation: Inspect the breach after the battle
- TalkTo: Report to Yenara

**New Monsters:**
- **Sand Wraith** (level 26-28) - Desert creatures that should not exist this far from the sands. Made of swirling sand and dark energy. Their presence confirms an underground ley line connects the desert to New Aeven.

**Details:** After the battle, inspection of the breach reveals the wraiths were drawn along the ley line. The seal in the cistern has cracked further. Urgency escalates.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP, Hitpoints XP

---

### Quest 6: "The Desert Beckons"

**Given by:** Archmage Yenara
**Level:** 28
**Summary:** Yenara uses the Resonance Lens in the cistern and discovers a map etched into the sealed wall, showing the primary seal deep in the desert. She arranges an expedition and sends you ahead.

**Objectives:**
- TalkTo: Speak with Yenara in the cisterns
- TalkTo: Meet Kael at the desert's edge

**Details:** Yenara reveals the Aetheri locked away something called "The Hollow King." The disturbances are aftershocks of the seals weakening. She has a contact in the desert - Kael, an Aetheri descendant who may know more. This quest transitions the player to the Desert zone.

**Rewards:** Gold, Magic XP

## Part 2: Desert Questline (7 Quests)

### Key NPCs

- **Kael** - Aetheri descendant living as a nomadic desert guide. Quiet, pragmatic, carries a staff covered in old Aetheri symbols. Knows the desert intimately but fears the deeper ruins. He's the last of his line and carries the burden of his ancestors' sacrifice.
- **Serah** - A treasure hunter who's been looting Aetheri surface ruins. Reckless, charming, and in over her head. Doesn't believe the "ancient evil" stories until she sees proof firsthand. Provides comic relief and a grounded perspective.

### Quest 7: "Shifting Sands"

**Given by:** Kael
**Level:** 28
**Summary:** Meet Kael at the desert's edge. He's wary but agrees to help after seeing Yenara's letter (or, if the player hasn't done the city chain, after explaining the tremors). He tests your ability to survive the desert.

**Objectives:**
- ReachLocation: Navigate to the Stone Pillars landmark
- ReachLocation: Navigate to the Buried Archway landmark
- ReachLocation: Navigate to the Sunken Colossus landmark
- KillMonster: Kill 10 Desert Scorpions
- KillMonster: Kill 5 Sand Vipers

**New Monsters:**
- **Desert Scorpion** (level 26-29) - Aggressive, venomous. Unusually agitated by the tremors.
- **Sand Viper** (level 28-30) - Fast, poisonous. Burrows in sand near ruins.

**Details:** Kael notes the creatures are more aggressive than usual - they can feel the tremors too. The landmarks introduce the player to the desert's visual storytelling: the Aetheri's ruins are everywhere, half-swallowed by sand.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP

---

### Quest 8: "Echoes of Aether"

**Given by:** Kael
**Level:** 30
**Summary:** Kael leads you to a surface ruin with a functioning Aetheri Obelisk. Using the Resonance Lens (or one Kael provides if the player skipped the city), you activate it and receive a vision.

**Objectives:**
- ReachLocation: Travel to the Aetheri Obelisk
- TalkTo: Use the Resonance Lens on the obelisk
- TalkTo: Discuss the vision with Kael

**The Vision:** A cinematic moment (dialogue/text sequence) showing the Aetheri civilization at its height - then the emergence of the Hollow King. A being of shadow that consumed every spell thrown at it, growing larger and more powerful. The Aetheri elders performing the sealing ritual, pouring their magic into three seals. The light fading from their eyes as their power was spent. The vision ends as the obelisk cracks.

**Details:** This is the major lore reveal. Kael fills in gaps - his ancestors passed down the story, but seeing it confirmed shakes him. The Hollow King is real, and it's waking up.

**Rewards:** Gold, Magic XP, Prayer XP

---

### Quest 9: "The Tomb Raider"

**Given by:** Serah (encountered at a ruin)
**Level:** 31
**Summary:** You encounter Serah at a partially excavated ruin. She's found an entrance to an underground Aetheri chamber but it's crawling with Sand Golems. She wants help clearing it for treasure.

**Objectives:**
- KillMonster: Kill 10 Sand Golems
- ReachLocation: Reach the inner chamber
- TalkTo: Examine the seal with Kael

**New Monsters:**
- **Sand Golem** (level 30-33) - Stone guardians still following ancient Aetheri orders. Slow but hit hard. High defense.

**Details:** Inside the chamber, there's no treasure - just the Second Binding Seal, heavily cracked and pulsing with dark energy. Serah is genuinely frightened for the first time. Kael arrives and identifies it as one of three seals keeping the Hollow King imprisoned.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP

---

### Quest 10: "Restoration"

**Given by:** Kael
**Level:** 33
**Summary:** Kael knows a ritual to reinforce the seals, but needs Aetheri Runestones - ancient crafted stones scattered across desert dig sites.

**Objectives:**
- ReachLocation: Travel to the Eastern Dig Site
- ReachLocation: Travel to the Western Dig Site
- ReachLocation: Travel to the Southern Dig Site
- KillMonster: Kill 3 Hollow Sentinels (one spawns at each dig site)
- CollectItem: Collect 3 Aetheri Runestones

**Mechanic:** Uses the existing dig site system. Each dig site spawns a **Hollow Sentinel** when the player digs.

**New Monsters:**
- **Hollow Sentinel** (level 33-35) - Echoes of the Hollow King's power seeping through the weakened seals. Dark, humanoid figures that drain mana on hit. Tough solo fights.

**Special mechanic:** Hollow Sentinels drain player mana with each successful hit, reflecting the Hollow King's magic-consuming nature. Encourages melee combat and potion use.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP, 3 Aetheri Runestones (quest items)

---

### Quest 11: "The Second Seal"

**Given by:** Kael
**Level:** 35
**Summary:** Travel to the second Binding Seal in a deeper desert temple to attempt the reinforcement ritual.

**Objectives:**
- ReachLocation: Enter the Desert Temple (instance)
- KillMonster: Kill 12 Hollow Shades
- ReachLocation: Reach the seal chamber
- TalkTo: Assist Kael with the ritual

**Instance: Desert Temple**
- A grand but decaying Aetheri temple. Walls ooze dark energy. Corruption is visibly spreading.
- Multiple rooms with Hollow Shades patrolling.

**New Monsters:**
- **Hollow Shade** (level 34-37) - Shadowy humanoids that patrol the temple. **Special mechanic:** Magic attacks heal Hollow Shades slightly, encouraging players to use melee/ranged combat. This directly reflects the Hollow King's magic-consuming nature and prepares players for the boss fight.

**The Twist:** Kael performs the ritual with the runestones... and it fails. The seal shatters. A booming voice echoes through the temple: *"Three seals. Two remain. One is already mine."* The first seal (beneath New Aeven) has already broken completely. The players were too late to save it.

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP, Prayer XP

---

### Quest 12: "The Last Seal"

**Given by:** Kael
**Level:** 37
**Summary:** One seal remains in the Aetheri Sanctum. The desert is changing - black sand near fissures, new Hollow creatures on the surface. Serah rejoins you with a crucial item.

**Objectives:**
- KillMonster: Kill 10 Hollow Devourers (new surface enemies)
- ReachLocation: Reach the Aetheri Sanctum entrance
- TalkTo: Use Serah's Aetheri Key to open the door

**New Monsters:**
- **Hollow Devourer** (level 36-38) - Larger, more dangerous Hollow creatures now roaming the desert surface. The Hollow King's influence is spreading rapidly.

**Details:** Serah has had a change of heart after seeing the second seal break. She gives you an **Aetheri Key** she looted from the first ruin - her "worthless trinket" turns out to be the only thing that can open the Sanctum door. Her line: *"I came here for treasure. Now I'd settle for the world not ending."*

**Rewards:** Gold, Combat XP

---

### Quest 13: "The Hollow King"

**Given by:** Kael
**Level:** 38-40
**Summary:** Enter the Aetheri Sanctum, fight through the Hollow King's forces, and face the ancient entity before the last seal breaks.

**Objectives:**
- ReachLocation: Enter the Aetheri Sanctum (instance)
- KillMonster: Kill 15 Hollow enemies (mixed Shades/Sentinels)
- ReachLocation: Activate 3 obelisks in the correct order (puzzle)
- KillMonster: Defeat The Hollow King (boss)
- TalkTo: Speak with Kael after the battle

**Instance: Aetheri Sanctum**
A grand underground temple - the heart of the Aetheri civilization. Half-consumed by dark energy. Multiple chambers lead to the final seal room. Three obelisks must be activated in the correct order (clues from earlier quest dialogue/lore) to open the path to the boss chamber.

**Obelisk Puzzle:** The order is hinted at through the Aetheri vision in Quest 8 and inscriptions found throughout the desert. A simple 3-step sequence that rewards players who paid attention to the story.

### Boss: The Hollow King

**Level:** 40
**HP:** High (scaled for a challenging solo fight at level 38-40)

**Mechanics:**
1. **Magic Resistance (Phase 1, 100%-50% HP):** The Hollow King has very high magic defense. Melee and ranged attacks are significantly more effective. This reinforces the lore that magic feeds him.
2. **Magic Drain (periodic AoE):** Every 15-20 seconds, the Hollow King casts Magic Drain - an area effect that saps player mana and heals him for a small amount. Players should keep distance when this is cast or accept the mana loss.
3. **Hollow Fragment Spawns:** Periodically spawns 2-3 small Hollow Fragment adds. If not killed within ~10 seconds, they merge back into the Hollow King and heal him significantly. Prioritizing adds is essential.
4. **Weakening Phase (50% HP):** Kael channels the Aetheri Runestones, breaking the Hollow King's magic resistance temporarily. Spells now deal full damage. This is the burn phase.
5. **Berserk Phase (25% HP):** The Hollow King abandons defense for offense - faster attacks, higher damage, but lower defense across the board. A DPS race to the finish.

**Resolution:** The Hollow King is defeated but not destroyed. Kael uses the last of the runestone energy to forge a new seal with the player's help. The Sanctum begins to collapse. The party escapes as the temple seals itself behind them.

On the surface, the tremors stop. The desert air feels lighter. Kael stays to watch over the seal - it's his birthright, after all. Serah heads to New Aeven with Aetheri artifacts, planning to fund future expeditions.

## Rewards Summary

### Per-Quest Rewards
Each quest grants appropriate XP (combat, magic, prayer, mining, hitpoints) and gold scaled to the level range.

### Chain Completion Rewards

**Aetheri Equipment Set (unique mid-tier gear):**
- **Aetheri Ward** (shield/off-hand) - Bonus magic defense, reflecting the "anti-magic" theme
- **Aetheri Blade** (weapon) - Deals bonus damage to Hollow-type enemies
- **Hollow King's Fragment** (rare material) - Dropped by the boss, used to craft a unique accessory at a New Aeven crafting station

**Zone Unlocks:**
- Desert zone fully accessible for slayer tasks and gathering
- Kael becomes a vendor selling desert supplies (potions, food, basic gear)
- Serah appears in New Aeven as a new NPC (future quest hook)

## Existing Systems Used

- **Dig sites** - Quest 10 uses dig site mechanic for Aetheri Runestone retrieval
- **Instances** - Three new instances for dungeon/boss content
- **All objective types** - KillMonster, CollectItem, TalkTo, ReachLocation
- **Crafting integration** - Quest 4 requires mined materials + monster drops for Resonance Lens
- **Quest items** - Resonance Lens carries between zones, Aetheri Key from Serah

## Future Hooks

- The Hollow King is sealed, not destroyed - potential for a harder endgame version
- Serah in New Aeven can give follow-up treasure hunting quests
- Kael as a desert quest hub for additional Aetheri exploration
- The broken first seal beneath New Aeven could have lasting effects on the city
- Other Aetheri ruins in unexplored desert regions
